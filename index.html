Amigo

This Machine offers contactless dispension for a Hygienic work environment where you have to not touch the dispenser to get the coffee.

Because of this feature of the machine, it will help you in the covid crisis.

Offering a wide range of cafe-style beverages with touch-free dispension, Amigo is the perfect fit for your workplace for coffee and more.

- In-built digital cleaning logs to ensure each brew is safe and Hygienic
